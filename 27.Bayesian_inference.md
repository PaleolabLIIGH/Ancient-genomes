### 27.3 Temporal signal assessment
Before constructing a time-calibrated tree, it's important to check whether the genetic sequences show molecular clock-like behavior.
Root-to-tip regression is a standard technique used to evaluate the temporal signal in genetic data.
This analysis should reveal a correlation between the sampling dates and the genetic distance from the root.
You have in this moment one rooted tree and other one without aougroup. For this step we are going to use the rooted tree:
![imagen](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2da22fca-c808-4e4d-b557-e8804321222e)

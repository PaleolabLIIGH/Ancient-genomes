### 27.2 Prepare your aligment
Having a very distant outgroup can also have deleterious effects on the estimated phylogeny (due to the so-called “long branch attraction” effect). Therefore, we can construct a new phylogeny 
after removing the outgroup in our aligment.

Download your mafft aligment to your local machine
